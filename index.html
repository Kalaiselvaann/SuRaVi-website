About Us

Founded on August 4, 2025, SuRaVi Brain Enrichment Center is a private educational company dedicated to nurturing children's intellectual, creative, and personal growth. With registered headquarters in Kanyakumari, Tamil Nadu, our vision is to shape future-ready kids and provide entrepreneurs with a meaningful business opportunity.
